Avatar: The Way of Water

*Avatar: The Way of Water* (2022) is the sequel to James Cameron's groundbreaking 2009 film *Avatar*. Set more than a decade after the events of the first film, the story follows Jake Sully (Sam Worthington) and Neytiri (Zoe Saldana) as they build their family and face new challenges on the beautiful moon of Pandora. The film explores the rich aquatic world of Pandora and introduces new cultures, as Jake and Neytiri struggle to protect their family and home from an impending threat. Known for its revolutionary visual effects and immersive underwater scenes, the movie continues the saga of the Na'vi people.

Released: 2022-12-16

Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi

Casts: Sam Worthington, Zoe Saldana, Sigourney Weaver, Stephen Lang, Kate Winslet

Duration: 192 min

Country: United States
