The Matrix

*The Matrix* is a groundbreaking 1999 science fiction film written and directed by the Wachowskis. The film follows Neo (Keanu Reeves), a hacker who discovers that the world he lives in is a simulated reality created by sentient machines to subjugate humanity. As Neo joins a group of rebels led by Morpheus (Laurence Fishburne), he uncovers his role in a larger fight to free humanity from the Matrix. Featuring mind-bending special effects and philosophical themes, *The Matrix* is a cultural touchstone.

Released: 1999-03-31

Genre: Action, Sci-Fi

Casts: Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, Carrie-Anne Moss, Hugo Weaving, Joe Pantoliano

Duration: 136 min

Country: United States

Production: Warner Bros. Pictures, Village Roadshow Pictures
