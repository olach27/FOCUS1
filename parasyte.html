Parasyte: The Anime

*Parasyte* follows Shinichi Izumi, a high school boy whose life takes a horrifying turn when parasitic creatures invade Earth and one attaches itself to his right hand. This gripping series explores the bond between host and parasite and poses questions about humanity's survival.

Released: 2014-10-08

Genre: Sci-Fi & Fantasy, Animation, Horror

Casts: Nobunaga Shimazaki, Aya Hirano, Kana Hanazawa, Daisuke Namikawa

Duration: 23 min

Country: Japan

Production: Madhouse
