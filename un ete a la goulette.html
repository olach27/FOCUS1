Un été à la Goulette

*Un été à la Goulette* is a 1996 Tunisian comedy-drama film directed by Férid Boughedir. The story takes place in the lively coastal district of La Goulette in Tunis, where three young people from different religious backgrounds—Muslim, Jewish, and Christian—are best friends. During one summer, they find themselves caught up in the tensions surrounding their respective cultures and religions. The film explores themes of friendship, love, and the cultural richness of Tunisia, all set against the backdrop of a vibrant and diverse society. It is both a nostalgic look at the past and a poignant commentary on the present.

Released: 1996-07-03

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Casts: Hichem Rostom, Najla Ben Abdallah, Mohamed Ali Ben Jemaa, Dora Bouchoucha

Duration: 90 min

Country: Tunisia

Production: Les Films du Sud, Centre National du Cinéma et de l'Image (CNCI)
