Your Name (Kimi no Na wa)

*Your Name* (Kimi no Na wa) is a beautifully crafted Japanese animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai. The story revolves around two teenagers, Taki and Mitsuha, who mysteriously begin to swap bodies and experience each other's lives. As they try to understand their strange connection, they uncover a deeper bond that transcends time and space. The film is a breathtaking mix of romance, fantasy, and drama, featuring stunning animation and a memorable soundtrack.

Released: 2016-08-26

Genre: Romance, Drama, Fantasy

Casts: Ryunosuke Kamiki, Mone Kamishiraishi, Tsubasa Honda, Nobunaga Shimazaki

Duration: 106 min

Country: Japan

Production: CoMix Wave Films
