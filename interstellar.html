Interstellar

*Interstellar* is a 2014 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story takes place in a future where Earth is facing environmental collapse. A group of astronauts embarks on a journey through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet. The protagonist, Cooper, played by Matthew McConaughey, is a former pilot turned farmer who is recruited for the mission. The film explores themes of time, space, love, and the survival of humanity, and is known for its breathtaking visuals, intricate scientific concepts, and emotional depth.

Released: 2014-11-07

Genre: Sci-Fi, Drama, Adventure

Casts: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain, Michael Caine

Duration: 169 min

Country: United States, United Kingdom

Production: Syncopy, Paramount Pictures, Warner Bros.
