Jujutsu Kaisen

*Jujutsu Kaisen* follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins the world of sorcerers to fight cursed spirits after swallowing a powerful curse. The anime is packed with intense battles, heartfelt moments, and stunning visuals, making it a must-watch for fans of action and supernatural stories.

Released: 2020-10-03

Genre: Action, Fantasy, Supernatural

Casts: Junya Enoki, Yuma Uchida, Asami Seto, Yuichi Nakamura

Duration: ~24 min/episode

Country: Japan

Production: MAPPA Studio
