El Harka

*Harka* tells the story of Tunisian youth trapped in poverty, exploring the harsh realities of their lives as they face the dangerous journey of illegal migration ("harka"). The series sheds light on social struggles, dreams, and the sacrifices made in pursuit of hope.

Released: 2023-03-23

Genre: Drama, Social Issues

Casts: Fethi Haddaoui, Rim Riahi, Mohamed Ali Ben Jemaa, Sonia Meddeb

Duration: ~45 min/episode

Country: Tunisia

Production: Shkoon Productions
