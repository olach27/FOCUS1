The Dictator

*The Dictator* (2012) is a political satire comedy film directed by Larry Charles, starring Sacha Baron Cohen. The film follows the story of General Aladeen, the dictator of the fictional North African country of Wadiya, who is forced to visit the United States after being removed from power. Struggling to maintain control, he faces a series of cultural misunderstandings, challenges to his authority, and a quest to bring democracy to his country. The film is known for its outrageous humor, political commentary, and the satirical portrayal of dictatorship and power.

Released: 2012-05-16

Genre: Comedy, Satire

Casts: Sacha Baron Cohen, Anna Faris, Ben Kingsley, Jason Mantzoukas

Duration: 83 min

Country: United States

Production: Four By Two Films, Sony Pictures Entertainment
