Noura's Dream

*Noura's Dream* (2019) is a Tunisian drama film directed by Hinde Boujemaa. The story follows Noura, a young woman living in Tunisia, struggling with a difficult and oppressive life. As she battles through the complexities of her relationship and her dream of finding freedom, Noura’s resilience is put to the test. The film explores themes of love, social justice, and the courage it takes to fight for a better life. It is a poignant reflection of the realities many women face in a patriarchal society.

Released: 2019-09-12

Genre: Drama

Casts: Hend Sabri, Hichem Yacoubi, Naim Badaoui

Duration: 100 min

Country: Tunisia

Production: Tunisian Films, Cinéma du Monde
