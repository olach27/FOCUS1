Paprika

*Paprika* is a 2006 Japanese animated science-fiction film directed by Satoshi Kon. The film explores the world of dreams and the blurred boundaries between reality and imagination. It follows Dr. Atsuko Chiba, a psychologist who uses a device that allows her to enter the dreams of her patients under the guise of her alter ego, Paprika. When a new dream technology falls into the wrong hands, it threatens to bring chaos to both the dream world and reality. The film is renowned for its surreal animation, philosophical themes, and stunning visual effects.

Released: 2006-11-25

Genre: Animation, Sci-Fi, Mystery, Drama

Casts: Megumi Hayashibara, Tôru Furuya, Kōichi Yamadera, Katsunosuke Hori

Duration: 90 min

Country: Japan

Production: Madhouse, Sony Pictures Classics
