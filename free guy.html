Free Guy

*Free Guy* is a hilarious and action-packed movie about a bank teller, Guy, who discovers he's a non-playable character (NPC) in an open-world video game. With his newfound awareness, he sets out to become the hero of his story while saving his world from deletion. Starring Ryan Reynolds, this movie is a blend of comedy, action, and heart.

Released: 2021-08-13

Genre: Comedy, Action, Sci-Fi

Casts: Ryan Reynolds, Jodie Comer, Taika Waititi, Joe Keery, Lil Rel Howery

Duration: 115 min

Country: United States

Production: 20th Century Studios, Maximum Effort
