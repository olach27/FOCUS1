El Andalib El Dokki

*El Andalib El Dokki* is a 2008 Egyptian romantic comedy-drama film directed by Sherif Arafa, starring the famous Egyptian actor, Ahmed Henidi. The film follows the life of a struggling singer named Ahmed, who dreams of becoming famous. He lives in the Dokki district of Cairo and faces numerous personal and professional challenges. As he works his way through obstacles, including his romantic entanglements, he learns the true value of love, friendship, and the pursuit of happiness. The film's themes focus on ambition, the struggle for success.

Released: 2008-11-30

Genre: Romance, Drama, Comedy

Casts: Ahmed Henidi, Menna Shalabi, Hany Salama, Sawsan Badr

Duration: 120 min

Country: Egypt

Production: Misr International Films, New Century Production
