Howl's Moving Castle

*Howl's Moving Castle* is a 2004 Japanese animated fantasy film directed by Hayao Miyazaki and produced by Studio Ghibli. The film follows Sophie, a young woman who is transformed into an old lady by a wicked witch's curse. Sophie seeks refuge in a magical, walking castle owned by Howl, a reclusive and mysterious wizard. As they journey through a world at war, Sophie and Howl uncover each other’s secrets and find courage in the face of danger. The film is renowned for its breathtaking animation, deep emotional story, and environmental themes.

Released: 2004-11-20

Genre: Fantasy, Adventure, Animation

Casts: Chieko Baishô, Tatsuya Gashûin, Takuya Kimura, Akihiro Miwa

Duration: 119 min

Country: Japan

Production: Studio Ghibli, Toho
