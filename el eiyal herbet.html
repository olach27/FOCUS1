El Eyal Kebret

*El Eyal Kebret* is a classic Egyptian comedy that tells the story of a family grappling with the father's infidelity. The children come together in a humorous and heartwarming way to save their family and protect their mother. With brilliant performances by Saeed Saleh, Ahmed Zaki, Younes Shalaby, Karima Mokhtar, and Hassan Mostafa, this movie remains one of the most iconic works in Egyptian cinema.

Released: 1979

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Casts: Saeed Saleh, Ahmed Zaki, Younes Shalaby, Karima Mokhtar, Hassan Mostafa

Duration: 120 min

Country: Egypt

Production: Egyptian National Cinema
